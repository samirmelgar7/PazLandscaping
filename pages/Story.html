Our Story

This company was built on hard work, integrity, and love for family. Our uncle founded this business with one simple goal: to provide for his family through honest work and dedication.

Day after day, year after year, he showed up—rain, heat, long hours— never cutting corners and never backing down from hard work.

His work ethic, resilience, and pride in doing things the right way are the foundation of everything we do today.

Every job we take on is done in his honor.

Continuing the Legacy

When Diego Paz passed away, the responsibility of the company was left to his son, Alejandro Paz. At just 20 years old, Alejandro stepped into a role he was never prepared for.

Through long days, hard lessons, and determination, the company continues to grow with the same values it was founded on.

This business is more than a job — it is a promise kept.
